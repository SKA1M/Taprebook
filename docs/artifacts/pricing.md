# Pricing — TapRebook plans

*Preserved from the original pilot collateral. All clinic pays its own BSP/Meta fees.*

## Lite — ₹2,999/mo

- WhatsApp reminders: T-24h (+ optional T-3h)
- Post-visit Google review request
- After-hours auto-reply
- Monthly KPI email (Kept, Rebooks, Reviews)
- Limit: up to 300 msgs/mo · 1 location
- Setup fee: ₹2,499 (one-time)

## Grow — ₹4,999–₹5,999/mo

- Lite +
- Malayalam templates (EN + MAL)
- "Running late" flow (hold `{{grace}}` min)
- No-show rebook nudge (2 slots or link)
- Manual-diary path (window → SLA 30–60min)
- Recall / Re-open & Treatment-plan follow-ups
- Custom time windows and smarter rebook rules
- Quarterly copy tuning + KPI snapshot
- Limit: up to 1,000 msgs/mo
- Setup fee: ₹3,999 (one-time)

## Plus — ₹8,999–₹12,999/mo

- Grow +
- Evening coverage (e.g., 6–9pm)
- Two-slot concierge
- Holiday/closure updates
- Priority no-show recovery
- KPI dashboard + monthly CSV export
- Cohorts/tags, staff alerts & SLAs
- Monthly ROI PDF + 1 A/B test
- Limit: up to 3,000 msgs/mo
- Setup fee: ₹6,999 (one-time)

## Multi-branch — from ₹19,999/branch/mo (floor ₹49,999 brand)

- Plus +
- Central templates with branch hours/links/overrides
- Centralized review/booking links; role-based access
- Dashboards with branch roll-ups · SLAs · monthly call

## Pilot Pledge

**14-day pilot: reduce no-shows ~10% or recover ≥2 appts, or we downgrade/comp next month.**
